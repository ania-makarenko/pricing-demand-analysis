# Pricing Strategy & Demand Optimization Analysis
## Overview
* This project analyzes how pricing impacts product demand and overall revenue performance. The goal is to simulate a real-world business scenario where a company must determine optimal pricing strategies to maximize revenue while accounting for customer demand behavior.
* Using SQL and Python, I explored pricing patterns, demand sensitivity, and revenue outcomes to generate actionable insights that could support data-driven pricing decisions.
## Business Problem
Pricing decisions directly influence both demand and revenue. Setting prices too high may reduce sales volume, while setting them too low may limit profitability.
This project answers:
* How does price impact demand?
* What price ranges maximize revenue?
* Which products are more price-sensitive?
* Where are there opportunities to improve pricing strategy?
## Tools Used
* SQL – data aggregation and KPI development
* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib/Seaborn) – data cleaning, analysis, and visualization
* Jupyter Notebook – analysis workflow
